What happens on your camera
stays on your camera.

Photoshoot processes every frame locally — in Electron on your desktop, or in your browser tab on the web. There are no uploads, no servers, no analytics, and no account. The web version keeps your gallery in your browser; the desktop version saves to your Pictures folder. That's it.

  • No cloud, ever
  • No tracking or analytics
  • Open-source & auditable
  • Community themes & filters are sandboxed
